I'm a second-year Master student in the Geometric Computing Lab at New York University, supervised by Prof. Daniele Panozzo and Prof. Daniel Zint. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University of Technology, advised by Prof. Jiazhou Chen.

My research interest is Computer Graphics. More specifically, I am working on Meshing and Elastic Simulation currently.

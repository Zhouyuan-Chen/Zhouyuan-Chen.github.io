Welcome to my website! My name is Zhouyuan Chen(could be pronounced like Jo-Yen Chan, you can also call me Cavers if it is easier for you to pronounce).

Currently, I'm a graduate student studying at the New York University Courant Institute, interested in Computer Graphics(especially for physically based simulation and geometry processing). I am fortunate to have the opportunity to work in the [NYU Geometric Computing Lab](https://cims.nyu.edu/gcl/people.html) now, supervised by Professor [Daniele Panozzo](https://cims.nyu.edu/gcl/daniele.html) and Professor [Daniel Zint](https://daniel-zint.github.io/). Before that, I was supervised by Professor [Jiazhou Chen](https://californiachen.github.io/) at Zhejiang University of Technology.

If you ask me why my favorite subject is Computer Graphics. The answer would be the most romantic thing I can imagine is using code to depict this world!
